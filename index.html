About

The 2023 Florida Gators football team represented the University of Florida in the Eastern Division of the Southeastern Conference (SEC) during the 2023 NCAA Division I FBS football season. Led by second-year head coach Billy Napier, the Gators played home games at Ben Hill Griffin Stadium in Gainesville, Florida.

Top Athletes

Graham Mertz

Graham Mertz (born December 6, 2000) is an American football quarterback for the Florida Gators. He previously played for the Wisconsin Badgers before transferring to Florida in 2023.

↑

Ricky Pearsall

Before transferring to Florida, Pearsall spent three seasons at Arizona State, enrolling there as a consensus three-star prospect from local Tempe (Ariz.) Corona Del Sol. In his final season with the Sun Devils, he broke out with 48 catches, 580 yards and four touchdowns.

Trevor Etienne

Trevor Etienne (born July 9, 2004) is an American football running back for the Georgia Bulldogs. He previously played for the Florida Gators. Etienne is the younger brother of NFL player Travis Etienne.
